The collection "The Trans-Atlantic Slave Trade Database" is divided into two Archival Units. The first AU contains a web crawl of images contained on the site, and the second the downloads available from the site, including an SPSS formatted copy of the Voyages database itself. The Names database is not included at this time.

This collection is growing gradually (biennial updates).
